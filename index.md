I am Sophie Suda and I work as a postdoc at the University of <a href="https://politikwissenschaft.philhist.unibas.ch/en/">Basel</a>! <br><br>

My research focuses on anything to do with parliaments, parties, or parties in parliament. I am also a member of the Steering Committee of the <a href="https://ecpr.eu/group/parliaments">ECPR Standing Group on Parliaments</a>.
Besides that, I am interested in governments, institutions, and anything quantitative. <br><br>

I am affiliated with the chair as well as an SNF project measuring the quality of MPs. Before my position in Basel, I was a PhD student at the University of <a href="https://ipk.uni-greifswald.de/en/">Greifswald</a>. I did my BA and MA at the University of <a href="https://www.sowi.uni-mannheim.de/en/">Mannheim</a>. Feel free to contact me if you have any further questions!
